Effortless Party Planning

A guided wizard walks you through every decision — theme, guest list, menu, co-hosts, and party rules — with an interface so intuitive it actually feels fun.

  • Add co-hosts and define party rules
  • Poll guests on menu and drink preferences

Invitations Made Simple

Auto-generate gorgeous digital invitations pulled straight from your phonebook, with real-time RSVP tracking built in.

  • Stylish, auto-generated invite designs
